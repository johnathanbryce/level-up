## SWE II interview process (composite — Glassdoor + JD signals)

Public data is thin (Diligent isn't FAANG-documented) and location/level-dependent. Reported as **fast + smooth, ~2.5 weeks, difficulty 3/5** — lighter than a FAANG loop. Typical shape:

1. **Recruiter screen** — experience discussion. (John effectively done.)
2. **Tech-fit / team-fit round with an Engineering Manager** — *more behavioral than technical.* Walks past experience; asks you to **compare frameworks and defend design decisions** ("why X over Y"). **The round that matters most to prep** — judgment + communication, not leetcode.
3. **Technical round + short coding exercise at the end** — practical coding, reasoning + clean code emphasized over trick algorithms.

## Stack they probe

- TypeScript/JavaScript incl. **OO design**
- Node, React, AWS, **serverless / event-driven**, REST APIs
- **Testing frameworks: Jest, Cypress, K6** ← notable emphasis (ties to John's planned `12-testing/` scaffold)
- IaC (CDK/Terraform) = "a plus," not a gate

## Caveats

- Most writeups are **Senior** SWE / non-Vancouver. One **Bengaluru** report had an in-person **paper** coding test — outlier, not Vancouver. If hit, request a virtual round with a team member.
- Diligent has been **modernizing** its loop; recent Vancouver process may differ. **Recruiter is the authoritative source** for exact Vancouver SWE II steps.
