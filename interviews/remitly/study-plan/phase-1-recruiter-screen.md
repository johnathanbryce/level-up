## How to Use This

1. **Sun-Mon:** read the topic notes once each. No drilling, just absorption.
2. **Tues:** cold self-quiz. For each topic, answer aloud: *"What is X? When would you reach for it? Why does Remitly care?"*
3. **Wed morning, 15 min before the call:** skim ONLY the "Likely recruiter prompts" sections. Don't try to re-read everything — that breeds anxiety, not fluency.

---

## Recovery rule (if asked about something not covered)

**Honest framing wins.** "I haven't worked hands-on with X, but I know it's used for [the thing] — I'd love to learn more about how the team uses it." Never bluff. A confident "I don't know, here's what I'd want to learn" beats a wobbly fake answer every time, and recruiters can smell the difference.
